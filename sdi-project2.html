SDI 1401: JOHN HERESHKO

Project 2

I have decided to continue the theme of fighting a dragon in this second project. It follows the path of a young knight who must face off against a ferocious dragon. And the dragon is not happy about it.

While the story does seem rather simple, it has gotten more complex since the last battle with the dragon.

JANUARY 16, 2014, JOHN HERESHKO
